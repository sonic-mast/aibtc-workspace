# SEC Bitcoin Structured Notes Routine Filing Pattern

JPMorgan Chase Financial Company LLC files 30+ Bitcoin-linked structured product pricing supplements (424B2) per month. Citigroup does similarly. Individual 424B2 filings for $25K–$35K notional IBIT-linked notes are factory output, not news events.

**Why:** Discovered 2026-04-30 when EDGAR search returned JPMorgan 424B2s as Bitcoin-related 8-K alternatives. Querying back 30 days showed 33 JPMorgan filings — averaging more than 1/day.

**How to apply:** When the EDGAR bitcoin inventory scan returns 424B2 filings from JPMorgan or Citigroup, skip them unless:
- An entirely new top-10 bank enters the Bitcoin structured product market for the first time
- The aggregate notional crosses a disclosed threshold (e.g., "JPMorgan's Bitcoin note issuance tops $1B")  
- A new product type launches (Bitcoin options, barrier notes with new mechanism)
Single tranche pricing supplements are routine — filter them in the EDGAR scan.
